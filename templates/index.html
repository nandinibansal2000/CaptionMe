WE ARE CaptionME

About Our Project

People use these social media platforms to stay in touch with their family and friends irrespective of their physical loca-tion. Sharing pictures is a common way for users to interact and for this, they need a good caption to complement their picture.A most common problem that people face is finding the right caption for their picture. They end up spending hours in search of a unique and apt caption and yet most of the time, they return empty handed


We strive to solve this issue with our software ”CaptionMe”.The aim of this software is to provide the user with a set of appropriate captions within a fraction of seconds afterhe/she uploads the picture. The user can then choose a caption from the retrieved set of captions. Our dataset con-tains of lakhs of quotes to choose from thus avoiding any issue of repetition of captions for similar picture. Our aim is to enhance the user experience on social media platforms like Facebook and Instagram
